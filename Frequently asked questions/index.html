    Frequently asked questions

    1. Q: Should i use my EA account to log in?

      1. A: No, you need to create a new account in-game and use it to login in the future.

    2. Q: Why I am getting kicked from the server?

      1. A: At the moment the only reason you can get kicked from the server is that you don't have PunkBuster installed. Check PunkBuster⁠

    3. Q: When launching multiplayer I am receiving "Please update the game to the latest version!" error?

      1. A: Update multiplayer using Official EA Patch

    4. Q: I am stuck infinitely on "Trying to connect...." in-game?

      1. A: Restart the game, when you get a login error hit retry and re-enter your login credentials.

    5. Q: Is there a way to change field of view (FOV)?

      1. A: Yes, navigate to your Documents\EA Games\Medal of Honor\Multiplayer\settings.ini. Open it with notepad and change Fov to your preffered value.

    6. Q: Is there a possibility to host my own server?

      1. A: At the moment, no. Project Neptune is still in development
